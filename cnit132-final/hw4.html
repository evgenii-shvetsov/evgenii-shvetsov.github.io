Young Genius Elementary School

  Young Genius Elementary School is one of the top-performing schools in the Bay Area.

  In this school, there are dual language programs, gifted programs, a Spanish Immersion Program, after-school programs, special education, and English as a second language programs.There are also online math and reading programs so that students can practice their skills at home and so parents will have a resource for helping their children. The curriculum is also available online. Students study health, technology, music, and P.E. in addition to the regular classroom subjects.
